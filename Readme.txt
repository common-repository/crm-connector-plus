WordPress to CRM/Helpdesk Integration.

== Description ==

CRM Connector Plus enables to capture Webforms to your CRM as Leads,Contacts,Products and SalesOrder.Embed forms as Posts & Widgets.

= Highlighted features =

* Data Bucket - Stores All Form, Users and WooCommerce Related Details based on Data Bucket Mapping.
* Generate forms with or without 3rd party web form.
* Embed forms in Page, Post or Widgets to capture CRM Lead.
* Captures potential leads to CRM.
* Redirects User to desired page after successful form submission.
* Notification on success or failure of Lead capture.
* Converts new Users as your CRM Contact. 
* Supports JoForce CRM.
* Sends Email Notification for Form Entries
* Customize Default Form.
* Customize the message shown on form submission.

**CRM ADDONS**

* Zoho CRM Pro Plus
* Vtiger Pro Plus
* Sugar Pro Plus
* Salesforce Pro Plus
* Suite CRM Pro Plus

**CRM Addons - Features** 

* Captures Leads, Contacts, Products and SalesOrder.
* Provided support for .com, .in and .eu domains (Zoho CRM)
* Users can move existing and new users to CRM modules via User Sync Addon.
* Users can sync both existing and new WooCommerce Products and Orders to CRM Products and SalesOrder,leads and contacts through  WooCommerce Sync Addon.

**HELPDESK ADDONS**

* FreshDesk Pro Plus
* Zendesk Pro Plus
* Vtiger Ticket Pro Plus
* Zoho Desk Pro Plus

**HELPDESK Addons - Features**

* Captures Tickets and Contacts.
* Addon users can move existing and new users to Helpdesk modules through User Sync Addon.
* Zoho Desk provides support for .com and .eu domains.

**FORM ADDONS**

* Ninja Form WP Pro Plus
* Qu Form Pro Plus
* Gravity Form Pro Plus
* Contact Form Pro Plus
* Caldera Form Pro Plus
* WP Form Pro Plus
* Leads Multi Forms Pro Plus

**Form Addons - Features**

* Create your own Lead/Contact form or Use existing webforms to capture your visitor info right inside your CRM. 
* Generate unlimited shortcodes and inject inside your post, pages and widgets.

*WooCommerce Addon**

* On enabling Woocommerce Manual Sync option, existing products / orders get synced with configured crm products or SalesOrder.
* On enabling User Auto Sync option, new products and orders get synced on creation.
* Provides support for Create, Update and Skip.

*Users Addon**

* On enabling User Manual Sync option, existing users get synced with configured crm / helpdesk modules.
* On enabling User Auto Sync option, new users get synced on creation.
